Welcome to the Human Wisdom Program
Benefits of Wisdom

We are developing an online program to help you discover your own wisdom so you can be happy and live with a sense of peace. This wisdom comes from understanding yourself and is not based on any ideology or belief. Wisdom enables you to go deeper and address the root cause of problems, rather than just deal with the symptoms.

  • Be Happy

  • Mentally Healthy

  • Have relationships without conflict

  • Meet life’s challenges without stress

  • Be emotionally intelligent

  • Live with compassion and a sense of peace

Wisdom Program for Children aged 5-11 years

This will be delivered by parents and teachers to the children. It includes ‘learning ladders’ a special program we have designed to share this wisdom with young children.

Through his conversations with children and teachers in primary schools Manoj has gained a unique insight into how to share this wisdom with children. He believes we completely underestimate their capacity to understand this wisdom, and feels if we can awaken this wisdom in them, it can be life-changing for them.

He also believes with a passion that children are the future of humanity, and if we can give them the gift of wisdom, they can meet life’s challenges differently.

Wisdom Program for Young Adults aged 12-17

This will be a self-learning program, with some input from parents and teachers as necessary. Adolescents face unique challenges in the world, and this program will aim to show them how wisdom can help them avoid and manage these challenges in a fresh way.

Manoj has worked in many secondary schools exploring wisdom with teenagers, and has found them amazingly receptive to this new approach of exploring the life they live.

Wisdom Program for Parents

Parenting isn’t easy and there is no guidebook. Many of us wish we knew then, what we know now.

This program aims to show how wisdom offers a different way to interact with children, manage the many challenges parents face in dealing with children, and offer tips on what a parent can do to share this wisdom with children.

Wisdom Program for Adults

This is our most detailed program, and offers adults of all ages the opportunity to discover their own wisdom and enjoy the many benefits it brings.

This wisdom can be life enhancing in a number of ways and allows us to be happy and live with a sense of peace. This is not based on any ideology or belief and comes naturally when we understand ourselves and how our minds work.
