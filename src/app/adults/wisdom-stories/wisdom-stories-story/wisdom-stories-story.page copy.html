Eating disorders are quite common, affecting up to 9% of the population worldwide (anad.org), and these patients have a higher mortality and suicide risk. The reasons for these disorders is complex and linked to an inability to deal with emotional pain or stress, body image issues and other challenges. If you live with a deeper understanding of yourself and how your mind works you are better equipped to deal with life’s challenges and be able to respond with wisdom. This can help you be comfortable in your own skin, and avoid and more easily overcome the challenge of eating disorders.
