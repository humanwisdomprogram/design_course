ABOUT THE AUTHOR

About Manoj Krishna

Manoj Krishna trained as a doctor in Pune, India before coming to the UK to pursue a career as a spine surgeon. He left that career to write the book Understanding Me, Understanding You- an enquiry into being human, and launch the non-profit Human Enquiry Project, which aims to transform individual lives, and make the world a better place.

He has had a deep interest in understanding how the human mind functions all through his life. He has facilitated dialogue groups where people would gather to enquire into the mind we all share. He feels passionately that we all need to be educated not only to understand the world around us, but also our inner spaces and how our minds work, and has worked with young people to explore this with them.

He has spoken in 48 institutions over the last 2 years, giving more than a 100 talks. His approach is simple. Since almost all human problems begin in our thinking, the solution also lies in understanding how our mind works, in a scientific way. Like computers which have different contents in their hard drives, but share the same operating system, we humans too are distinct in looking different and having unique memories, but hidden from our awareness, our minds function in similar ways. The nature of fear and pleasure is the same in all of us for example, so we can explore it together. It is like looking at the same moon together, and sharing what we discover. This understanding awakens intelligence and as a result we can meet life’s challenges with serenity, have relationships without conflict and live with compassion and a sense of peace.

The work he does is free. For more information visit humanenquiry.org, and follow the project on social media where the tag is humanenquiry.
